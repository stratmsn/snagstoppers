What are SnagStoppers?

SnagStoppers are an elegant solution to an ageless problem: How do you keep your lines from snagging everything in sight when not in the water? The answer: SnagStoppers! SnagStoppers wrap around and protect your lines, your clothing, and your lures. Made of a durable all-weather vinyl, SnagStoppers are your affordable, convenient solution for preventing unintended puncture wounds. Ask for more details!
